Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be an ASL performance?

American Sign Language interpreters will be at the matinee performance on Saturday, Dec. 8.

Is there a waiting list for tickets that might become available?

There is no ticket waiting list. Continue to check in at peacecenter.org.

When will Hamilton be at the Peace Center?

Hamilton will be at the Peace Center Dec 4-16, 2018 for a limited two-week engagement.

What is Hamilton about?

Hamilton is the story of America’s founding father Alexander Hamilton, an immigrant from the West Indies who became George Washington’s right-hand man during the Revolutionary War and was the new nation’s first treasury secretary. Featuring a score that blends hip-hop, jazz, blues, rap, R&B and Broadway, Hamilton is the story of America then, as told by America now.

What is the show’s run time?

Hamilton runs 2 hours and 45 minutes with a 20 minute intermission.

What is the age recommendation for Hamilton?

Hamilton is recommended for ages 10+. The show contains some strong language and non-graphic adult situations.

Where can I learn more about Hamilton?

www.HamiltonMusical.com
Facebook: HamiltonMusical
Instagram: HamiltonMusical
Twitter: @HamiltonMusical

Will Lin-Manuel Miranda and the original cast be in the show?

No, this will be an all new cast, but it will be produced with the same talent, attention to detail and high quality as the Broadway production.

When is Hamilton coming back to Greenville?

Many factors are considered when presenting a national tour, including availability and routing. We don’t have a confirmed return date scheduled, but we want to bring the show back to Greenville as soon as possible.

Will there be an EDUHAM performance?

No. Due to the limited two-week engagement, an EDUHAM performance will not be possible in Greenville.

Will there be a lottery for Hamilton?

Yes, there will be a digital lottery in Greenville. Additional details will be released closer to the engagement.

Are there any discounts for students/educators/military?

There are no discounts available for Hamilton.

When will season ticket holders see the show?

Season ticket holders will be placed in the first week of performances, Dec. 4-9, unless you have exchanged into a different performance.

What if I can’t find my tickets or they get lost in the mail?

Call 864.467.3000 or visit the box office in person at the Peace Center.

Can I exchange my tickets for another performance?

Due to demand, there are no exchanges for Hamilton. You are encouraged to make your plans around your Hamilton date. All tickets to performances at the Peace Center are non-refundable.
