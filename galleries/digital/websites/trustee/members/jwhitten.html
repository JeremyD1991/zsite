Jim Whitten

With degrees in Science and Architecture from Georgia Tech, Jim Whitten completed a 35 year career with Fluor Corporation retiring as Vice President Operations in 2005. As a community volunteer, Jim has served on the boards of several Greenville arts organizations, and is currently serving on the Greenville Spartanburg Airport Environs Commission overseeing zoning around the airport. Jim and his wife Beverley were married in May 1965 and live in the Collins Creek area of Greenville.
