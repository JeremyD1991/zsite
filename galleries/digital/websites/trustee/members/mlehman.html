Melinda Lehman

CEO of Happen Associates, Inc.

Melinda is a Partner with Happen, a global innovation firm and the founder of Happen North America. Melinda has 20+ years of experience in consumer and customer marketing and strategy, with particular expertise in customer experience, new product innovation and brand positioning.

While passionate about all areas of innovation, Melinda’s expertise lies in insight-led marketplace potential. She enjoys working in a wide range of industries including publishing, FMCG, retail, financial services, and pharmaceuticals.

Prior to becoming one of North America’s leaders in catalyzing innovation, Melinda held senior marketing, research, and innovation consulting positions with Procter & Gamble, Pfizer, Kraft Foods and Sklar Wilton & Associates. She has designed and facilitated hundreds of innovation programs, is a certified Practitioner of Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP), and is trained in timeline therapy and hypnotherapy.

Melinda has an MBA from The Richard Ivey School of Business and a B.A.S. from York University. She runs Graduate Level Innovation Skills Programs at Ivey Business School. She is on the Ivey Council for Entrepreneurship and the PDMA Council. Melinda’s dedication and focus is reflected both in her work and in her personal life. When Melinda is not busy leading innovation work, she enjoys cycling, running and swimming. She recently completed her third Iron Man. She is a founding member of Les Domestiques, a philanthropic movement committed to promoting a safe and vibrant culture of cycling in Canada.
