Bruce Holstien

President & CEO of Spartanburg Regional Healthcare System

Bruce has served as the CEO of these organizations since 2011. Previously, he served as the CEO of the six-hospital Christus Spohn Health System in Texas and as SVP of Sentara Healthcare in Virginia. He holds a Bachelors of Science in Health Administration an MBA and is completing his Masters in Gerontology. He is one of the few executives in his field to have worked in the not-for-profit, for-profit and government sectors of health; and served in senior posts within the hospital, medical group and insurance industries. Interestingly, his father served in the US Navy Medical Corp as a Hospital Administrator. His SC family roots date back to the 1700â€™s to Edgefield County, South Carolina. Bruce has worked in nearly every department of a hospital beginning as a volunteer at the age of 14 and his first paying job at the age of 16 washing dishes in a hospital dietary department. Other than the major duties of his day job, Bruce is determined to help revolutionize the current approach to clinical trials to increase novel treatment access to underrepresented populations as well as speed the rate of discovery for new therapies. In his spare time, he enjoys spending time with his two sons, Sean and Travis; and enjoys golf, fishing and hunting.
