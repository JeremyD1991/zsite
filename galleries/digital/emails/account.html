Wait, what is My Account?
My Account is the Peace Center’s online ticketing portal, which you can access anytime here. But it is more than just a way to purchase tickets online. It allows you to manage your information, view your purchases, change your password, and more.

How / where do I log in?
Click here to be taken directly to the log in page. You can always find My Account in the top bar navigation while exploring peacecenter.org.

Are you sure I have one?
Yes! Your account is linked with your email address. The address that received this email is your username.

What if I forgot my password?
No worries! Simply click “Forgot Your Password?” on the login page. To reset your password, type in your email address on file. A new temporary password will be sent to you. If you do not receive the email, contact the Box Office at 864.467.3000.

What can I do to make sure my account is ready to go?
  • Update your address and add account preferences
    From your My Account menu, click on “Account Information.” From there, make sure your contact information and address are up to date. You can even customize your interests to tailor the emails you receive!
     
  • Save/update a payment method
    Make purchasing tickets even easier by saving a payment method. From your My Account menu, click on “Saved Payment Methods.” Click “Add Payment Method” to tie a credit card to your account. This will help save you time when completing online ticket purchases.

    Already saved a payment method? Check to make sure your attached credit card is not expired.
