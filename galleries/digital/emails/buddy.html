The legendary Buddy Guy will be here next week!

He needs no introduction…
Watch Guy chat about the origins of Chicago Blues with David Letterman on My Next Guest Needs No Introduction.

“The Last Bastion of the Blues…”
Forbes recently profiled Guy when his latest album – The Blues is Alive and Well – dropped this summer. One song closes with “My friends said before they died, to keep the blues alive.” Guy expands on that sentiment in the interview remarking, “We knew one day, one of us was gonna leave the others here. My late friends, Junior Wells, Muddy Waters, B.B. King, Howlin’ Wolf, all of ‘em would say things like that.”

Dive into his incredible life story…
The American Roots Music series interviewed Guy for their Oral Histories project. Read the full transcript to learn about everything from his humble beginnings as a sharecropper’s son to what he hopes to live long enough to accomplish.

In case you need a refresh on the hits before hearing them live…
Listen to all his essential tracks in Spotify’s “This is Buddy Guy” playlist.
