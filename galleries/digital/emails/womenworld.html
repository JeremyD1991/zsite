Oct 31 – Nov 2 • 9:45 AM & 11:45 AM • Gunter Theatre • $10
 
About the Show
Open your students’ minds to the vastness of world cultures in one spellbinding performance. Hailing from Japan, Italy, India, and Haiti, Women of the World is a vocal ensemble from different corners of the globe that comes together to sing original compositions and folk music in over 17 languages. The group spreads awareness of unique musical genres, cultures, and philosophies. Promoting world unity, diversity, and peace, this concert brings the world to your students without them ever having to leave Greenville!

Connect the Show to Your Classroom
  • Learn to speak Japanese, Haitian Creole, and Italian with the ensemble through their educational YouTube playlist.
  • Take a closer look at some traditional Japanese and Haitian folk songs with our educator guide and corresponding student worksheets.
  • Explore the countries represented by the artists through these recommended books.
