How to Buy
Tickets to Hamilton go on sale Saturday, September 8, at 10 am, and there are two ways to purchase:
  • In person at the Peace Center Box Office, located at 300 S. Main St. in Downtown Greenville. (Line will start at 7 am).
  • Online at tickets.peacecenter.org/Hamilton.
Tickets will not be available by phone.

Being in line online or in person does not guarantee that you will have the opportunity to purchase tickets. Sales will end when all available seats have been allocated. There is a four (4) ticket limit per account.

How to Prepare
If purchasing tickets online, make sure your account information is up-to-date by clicking here. If you have purchased tickets with us before, you have an account linked to this email address.
